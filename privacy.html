Privacy policy

Updated: August 16, 2023

Thank you for visiting TheJobsfy.com ("us", "we", or "our") for your job search needs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We want to assure you that we collect only limited and non-personal information to enhance your job search experience and provide relevant services. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other sensitive data.

Information Collected Through Search Form

When you use the search form on our website to find jobs, we may collect certain non-personal information to improve our services. This information may include keywords used in the search, the type of job you are looking for, and your general location (city, state, or region). This data is solely used to provide you with relevant job search results and to enhance our website's functionality.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain non-personal information is automatically collected to help us understand how visitors use our site. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, access times, and other technical information. This data is used solely for statistical purposes and does not identify any individual user.

This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, access times, and other technical information. These third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies to gather this data, which is used solely for the intended purposes of the respective services.

Managing Your Data

You can customize your experience with third-party services and manage your data preferences directly through the respective service providers. To learn more about how these services use data and how to control your information, please review the privacy policies and settings provided by the respective service providers.
